Rule Reminders

Advanced Game
  • Advance & Attack by moving forward with one card, then attacking with another.
  • Defenders may parry or retreat when facing an advance & attack.
  • Retreating uses a card to move backward; the attacker keeps the initiative.
  • Successful parries force the defender to act before drawing back to five cards.
  • Deck exhaustion triggers a final attack opportunity before determining distance winners.
