Rule Reminders

Basic Game
  • Move exactly the value of the card forward or backward.
  • Cards cannot move a swordsman off the piste or through the opponent.
  • Match the distance to attack. Attacks in Basic mode always hit.
  • A round ends on a hit, no legal moves, or when the deck is exhausted.
Standard Game
  • Attackers may stack identical cards to strengthen an attack.
  • Defenders parry with the same number of cards totaling the attack value.
  • Successful parries require taking a turn before drawing new cards.
Advanced Game
  • Advance & Attack by moving forward with one card, then attacking with another.
  • Defenders may parry or retreat when facing an advance & attack.
  • Retreating uses a card to move backward; the attacker keeps the initiative.
